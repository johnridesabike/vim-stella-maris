		  "Stella Maris" color scheme    by John

A color scheme for our Star of the Sea.

==============================================================================
Color scheme options					stella-options*

						*g:stella_transp_bg*
Set to 1 if you want a transparent background. Takes effect only in the
terminal.
>
	let g:stella_transp_bg = 0
<
